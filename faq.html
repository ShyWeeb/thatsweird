FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

What is ThatsWeird ? ▼

ThatsWeird is a website that shows random videos from a database to users, these videos can be anything deemed out of the ordinary, like art projects, out of context clips or horror content. Of course, I don't mean it negatively, one person might think something is weird while another doesn't.

The goal of this website is to show content that (hopefully) hasn't been seen by the user before.

Where do the videos come from ? ▼

Most of them come from YouTube playlists, but I also add content that I've discovered as well as suggestions.

How can I suggest content ? ▼

To suggest content, please send the link or links to submit@thatsweird.xyz. Currently, the content needs to be hosted on YouTube and not contain any of the following.

- Nudity
- Inappropriate, offensive or illegal content
- Made for the sole purpose of promoting any online services or accounts (weird ads and arg videos are allowed)
- Re-upload or already shown here

I reserve the right to deny any suggestions whether the content broke the following or not.

Why is video A not here anymore ? ▼

Usually, this can happen for one of the following reasons.

1. A was directly removed from the external platform in question (such as YouTube).
2. A was removed from ThatsWeird after a request from the copyright holder, creator or uploader.
3. A was removed from ThatsWeird because the content in question is inappropriate, offensive, illegal or has no reason to be here.
4. A was removed from ThatsWeird for ethical reasons.

How can I ask a question that isn't listed here ? ▼

For any questions, please email help@thatsweird.xyz.
